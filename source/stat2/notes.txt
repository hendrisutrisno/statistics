Week 1
Compact title: Sampling Dist I
Core topics: random sampling; key statistics (x̄, s², p̂) as RVs; sampling distribution concept; standard error as “typical error.”
Lab: simulation of x̄ and p̂ for multiple n; visualize SE shrinking.
Reading: B1 Required Ch8.1–8.3; B2 Recommended 8.1–8.3; B3 Optional 4.1; B4 Optional.

Week 2
Compact title: Sampling Dist II (CLT)
Core topics: CLT; when it works; practical normal approximation logic; interpreting “approximately Normal.”
Lab: CLT simulation under skew/heavy tails; compare tail areas across n.
Reading: B1 Required Ch8.4; B2 Recommended 7.2 + 8.4; B3 Optional 4.4.1–4.4.2; B4 Optional.

Week 3
Compact title: Sampling Dist III (t/χ²/F + Plots)
Core topics: sampling distribution of s²; t-distribution; F-distribution; quantile/probability plots as diagnostics.
Lab: t vs z practice; QQ/probability plots; “what deviations matter” checklist.
Reading: B1 Required Ch8.5–8.8; B2 Recommended 8.5–8.8; B3 Optional 4.6.1–4.6.3; B4 Optional.
